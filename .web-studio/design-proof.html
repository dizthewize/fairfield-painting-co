FAQ Accordion

How much does it cost to paint a house in Fairfield, CA?+
Pricing varies by square footage, prep work, and paint grade. Most interior rooms run $400–$900, and full home exteriors typically range $4,500–$9,500. We send a fixed-price written estimate within one business day of your walk-through — no surprises.
Do you provide the paint, or do I?+
We do. We work primarily with Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore premium lines, and your estimate includes all paint, primer, and materials.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Yes. We're fully licensed in California and carry general liability plus workers' comp. We're happy to send certificates before the job.
